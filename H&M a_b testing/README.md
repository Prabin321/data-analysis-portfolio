## 📌 Project Overview
In this project, I acted as a **Junior Data Analyst on H&M’s Marketing Team**, analyzing an A/B test designed to improve email campaign performance during a seasonal promotion.

The goal was to determine whether modifying the subject line could increase customer engagement, measured by email open rate.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
H&M aimed to boost engagement and sales during their **Summer Deal Days campaign**. The marketing team tested whether adding urgency to the subject line would increase email open rates.

---

## 🧪 Experiment Setup

Two versions of the same promotional email were tested:

| Group   | Subject Line                                      |
|--------|--------------------------------------------------|
| Control | 20% off + free shipping                         |
| Test    | Your last chance to score 20% off + free shipping |

The only difference between the two groups was the subject line.

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- `group` → control or test  
- `subject_line` → email variation  
- `opened` → 1 (opened) or 0 (not opened)  

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

### 1. Hypothesis Definition
- **H0 (Null):** Changing the subject line has no effect on open rate  
- **H1 (Alternative):** The new subject line increases open rate  

---

### 2. Sample Design
- Historical open rate: **18%**
- Target improvement: **20%**
- Required sample size: **~9,300 emails**

---

### 3. Data Aggregation (PivotTable)

| Group   | Emails Sent | Emails Opened |
|--------|------------|---------------|
| Control | 4,764      | 836           |
| Test    | 4,673      | 894           |

---

### 4. Performance Comparison

- Control Open Rate: **17.55%**
- Test Open Rate: **19.13%**
- Lift: **~9% improvement**

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- The urgency-based subject line outperformed the control group  
- The test group achieved higher engagement despite a slightly smaller sample size  
- Results were statistically significant, supporting the effectiveness of the new strategy  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendation
H&M should implement the subject line:

👉 **"Your last chance to score 20% off + free shipping"**

This version improves open rates and increases customer engagement.

To fully evaluate impact, future analysis should include:
- click-through rate (CTR)
- conversion rate
- revenue per email

---

## ⚠️ Risks & Considerations
- Timing effects (weekday vs weekend sends)
- Audience segmentation differences
- External campaign influences
- Open rate does not directly measure revenue impact
